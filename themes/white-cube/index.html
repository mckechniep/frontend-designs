About

The less there is in the frame, the more there is to see.

Based in Tokyo. Working in large format and medium format film. Drawn to architecture, empty spaces, and the geometry of ordinary things.

Focus Fine art, architecture, interiors
Exhibited Tokyo Photographic Art Museum, Foam Amsterdam, ICP New York
Medium 4x5 large format, Hasselblad 500CM
