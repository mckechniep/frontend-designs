# Golden Example: App Screen / Arctic Mono

## Scope Lock

- output: `nextjs`
- styling: `css`
- profile: `arctic-mono`
- theme: `dark`

## Signature

- research workspace or mission-control screen with numbered modules and technical side notes
- strong mono utility labels, measured editorial headings, and sharp geometry
- cold surface layering with blueprint-like annotation without becoming corporate-blueprint
- form and review flows should feel lab-grade, precise, and calm
